FAQ

How does GlowUp Daily help me improve?

It guides your daily routines across fitness, mindset, looks, and social skills with structured tasks, guided workouts, and an AI coach.

Do I need an account?

Yes — sign in with Google or email/password to sync your progress across devices.

What data do you store?

Your profile, goals, tasks, journal, and nutrition logs are stored in Firebase. You control your data and can export/delete it in the app’s data management section.

How does the AI work?

The app uses a secure, Firebase-authenticated proxy to Google’s Gemini model. Your requests are tied to your account for safety and abuse protection.

Will it work offline?

You can view and log key items offline; changes sync when you’re back online. AI features and some content require internet.

How do streaks and reminders work?

Smart reminders schedule at helpful times; streaks reward consistent daily completion. You can adjust notifications in Settings.

How do I manage my subscription?

The app uses a secure, Firebase-authenticated proxy to Google’s Gemini model. Your requests are tied to your account for safety and abuse protection.

Can I change my email or password?

Yes. Email change is securely approved via your current email; password changes require re‑authentication.
